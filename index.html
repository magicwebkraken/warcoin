How to Get Started

New to crypto? No wallet yet? Follow these four steps to join the WAR army.

01
Get Metamask
Head to Metamask — and Install Metamask in your browser.
02
Create a Wallet
Click "Connect Wallet" and sign up using your email or social login. Metamask will create a Ethereum wallet for you automatically.
03
Fund Your Wallet
Deposit ETH into your new wallet. You can buy ETH on any major exchange (Coinbase, Binance, etc.) and transfer it to your wallet address.
04
Buy $WAR
Search for the $WAR token using the contract address, enter the amount of ETH you want to swap, and confirm the transaction. You're now enlisted.

Pro Tip

Always keep a small amount of ETH in your wallet for transaction fees (gas). And never share your private keys or seed phrase with anyone — that's the one order you never disobey.
